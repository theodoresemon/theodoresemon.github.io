About this Website

Hello and welcome! I am a sexuality researcher turned data scientist. As a data scientist with a social science background, I made this website for people who want to utilize their analytical skill set in the business world, but have no idea where to start. Or perhaps you are interested in data science but are intimidated by the computer programming and digital marketing requirements - skill sets that most social scientists know nothing about. Never fear! If you have a solid foundation of statistics and a passion for data, than you have the most important requirements as a data scientist. This website will tell you what skills you need to learn, how to learn them, and how you can apply them in the field.

About Me

I am the head of digital analytics at Creative Agency Chicago (CACH). CACH is a group of creative freelancers from around the world that specialize in corporate identity, website development, e-commerce, and marketing strategies. In short, my department aims to glean consumer insight to optimize product performance.

From Kinsey to KPIs

When I enrolled in the clinical psychology doctoral program at Northwestern University in 2012, my end goal was to join the academic ranks of the famous sexuality researchers that came before me. After all, I had the privilege of working with one of the greatest minds in the field - Michael Bailey. However, halfway through my doctoral program, I began to question whether academia was the right fit for me when I became involved with CACH by happenstance.

For a year, I peripherally watched a group of my friends build CACH from the groud up. I learned how account managers, web-designers, and web-developers interacted to produce digital content. In contrast to academia, CACH was fast-paced and success was based on collaboration and networking. One successful project would lead to a referral for another. The growth of CACH imbued my entrepreneurship spirit.

During this time, a friend in my doctoral program introduced me to the field of data science. As a recently hired data scientist, he showed me how to supplement my analytical skills for the digital market. In 2015, I created the analytics department at CACH. My success at CACH in the past year and my advisor's blessing lead me to leave academia in 2016.

I believe that my unique path to the world of data science is what sets me apart from other data scientists. The areas of the field in which I need more experience are balanced by my strong communication and analytical skills I refined in the four years I trained as an empirically-oriented researcher. As a result, I am confident and eager to exceed the needs of my clients.
